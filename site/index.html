Each god is unique!

Apart from that, gods are also separated by the type of Power they utilize: NewUI Type Physical.png Physical or NewUI Type Magical.png Magical. Hunters, Warriors and Assassins use Physical Power, Lifesteal and Penetration while Guardians and Mages use Magical Power, Lifesteal and Penetration. Both abilities and basic attacks scale off power and deal Physical or Magical damage. Gods are restricted from acquiring items that provide stats of the opposite power type and will not benefit from any buff or aura that provides said stats. Unless otherwise stated, players cannot choose the same god a teammate has already picked. In Ranked and draft pick modes, only one player in either team can pick a certain god.
